In Loving Memory

Linus Ngigi Karanja

1940 - 9/11/2025

Forever in our hearts, your kindness and light live on.

life story

Birth

The late Linus Ngigi Karanja was born in the year 1940 at Karuthi Village, Maria-ini Sub-Location, Murang'a County. His parents are the late Karanja Wanuthia and Prisilla Muthoni. His siblings are the late Gerald Mburu, the late Keziah Wanjiku, the late James Mwaura, Samson Mungai, Bonface Mburu and the late Mercy Wanjiku.

Childhood Life and Education

The Late Linus Ngigi Karanja was brought up well as per the children of his time and when he was of age he helped his parents with home chores. In the year 1948 he joined Kihuru-ini primary school up to the year 1956 when he sat for his KAPE.

Work

In the year 1964 the Late Linus Ngigi Karanja was employed by the Nairobi City Council up to the year 1995 when he retired. Later he backed home and ventured into farming business a venture he has continuously involved himself with until his demise.

Church

The Late Linus Ngigi Karanja joined ACK Kihuru-ini as a young person and was later baptized and confirmed in the same church. He has been an active enrolled member of the KAMA Department of the same church till his demise.

Marriage

The Late Linus Ngigi Karanja met his beloved wife Grace Wamaitha and got married in the year 1963. They were blessed with five children namely: Joseph Karanja, Nancy Muthoni, Samuel Mungai, Julius Mburu and Lucy Wambui. Later, in the year 1999 they solemnized their marriage at ACK Kihuru-ini.

Demise

The Late Linus Ngigi Karanja has enjoyed good health for a long period but on Sunday 9th November 2025 he was involved in a fatal motorcycle road accident and was rushed to Kenol Hospital where he succumbed to injuries while receiving treatment. The Late Linus was 85 years old and he has left behind his loving wife, 5 children, grandchildren and great grandchildren. May his soul rest in eternal peace.
